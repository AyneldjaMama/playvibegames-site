Privacy Policy – Vibe Games

Last updated: March 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile games and apps published by Vibe Games ("the Apps," "we," "us," or "our"), operated by Sean Harding, handle information when you use them.

1. Information We Collect

Vibe Games apps do not directly collect or store any personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or any other personal details to use our apps.

The apps may store the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your high scores and game statistics

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

2. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

Some Vibe Games apps use Google AdMob to serve rewarded video advertisements. AdMob may collect and use certain information to provide personalized ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Device type, operating system, and language settings
  • Ad interaction data (e.g., whether an ad was viewed or clicked)

This data is collected by Google, not by us, and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization
  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

3. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through our apps, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@playvibegames.com
